### Goals

### Values

* Classically futuristic (think Verne), not vintage or retro
* Classy, not bougie
* Explorative, not authoritative
* Curious, not know-it-all
* Story-based, not purely theoretical
* Well-reasoned, not emotional

5. **What do you believe that all your competitors disagree with?**
    * Claims should be nuanced
    * "The Science" doesn't have all the answers --- not every claim needs a
      study backing it up, and not all claims that have them are legitimate
      (especially in psychology)
    * Story is a big part of how humans communicate deeply
6. **What values do you believe so strongly that you're willing to lose money
   rather than violate them?**
    * Preserving and promoting user agency is more important than making money
    * Individual privacy should be respected
    * Claims should be given appropriate nuance
    * Business incentives should be structured to minimize perverse incentives
      and prioritize user wellbeing
